About us

Jibestream, an Inpixon company, is the premier indoor mapping and location platform provider for enterprise organizations. We provide a geospatial platform that allows you to solve mission critical problems by integrating business data with indoor maps.

We are transforming the way people engage with indoor spaces. Every day, organizations rely on us to power their indoor maps creating an enriched experience of place. With a portfolio of high-profile venues deployed globally, including the Pentagon, World Trade Center, Majid Al Futtaim retail complexes, various Department of Defense hospitals and hundreds of other buildings in both the private and public sector, we are the go-to leader in the indoor mapping space.


An Inpixon company

In 2019, Jibestream was acquired by Inpixon (Nasdaq: INPX), a leader in Indoor Positioning Analytics (IPA). A pivotal component to Inpixon's offering, the Jibestream's platform provides a key foundational layer enabling the visualization of indoor location data. Together, Jibestream's mapping and location technology and Inpixon's indoor data technology, sensors, video surveillance solutions, and GPS offerings, combine to deliver actionable indoor location data and intelligence. For more information about Inpixon, visit inpixon.com.

Our approach

We believe that a successful indoor mapping journey begins with education. Our in-depth knowledge and understanding of the indoor mapping and location market combined with our hands-on approach will help you build a strong foundation for success.

Historically, the world has looked at maps as flat static images. To us, a map is not a picture - it’s dynamic, it’s multi-dimensional, it’s addressable. We look at maps as layers and objects that can be associated with any third-party data source so you can easily find, analyze and see, people, places and things - on an indoor map.
